Social Graphics

Do this once at the start of every ChatGPT session. This teaches ChatGPT who you are, what the brand looks like, and how to think — before you ask it to create anything.

Step 1
Upload your files first

Before typing anything, upload both of these into the chat using the attachment button.

  • Your professional headshot — used on agent intro, market updates, and contact slides
  • The Limitless Group logo — upload a version with a background (white or cream). Transparent PNG files cause rendering issues. If you only have a transparent version, screenshot it on a white background first, or screenshot it from any Limitless marketing material.

💡 One upload per session. ChatGPT remembers both files for every graphic you create in that conversation.

Step 2
Send your intro + brand instructions

After uploading, send this message with your real details filled in. This is the foundation ChatGPT uses for the entire session.

I'm going to have you create a series of Instagram graphics for me today. Before we start, here's everything you need to know: MY INFO: Name: [YOUR FULL NAME], REALTOR® Phone: [YOUR PHONE NUMBER] Email: [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] Instagram: @mylimitlessagent Brokerage: Limitless Group I've uploaded my headshot and the Limitless Group logo above. Reference them whenever an agent photo or logo is needed — don't generate a fake logo or placeholder. BRAND STYLE — follow these rules for every single graphic: - Always use a real estate photo as the background — an interior or exterior of a home. Never use a plain, solid, or abstract background. - If the background photo competes with the text, blur it, fade it with an overlay, or both. The photo should always feel present but never overwhelming. - Color palette: warm white (#faf8f5), soft cream (#f3efe9), warm gold (#9b8c6e), and deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) only. No blues, greens, purples, or any other colors. - Typography: bold condensed all-caps for headlines, wide letter-spacing for labels and subtext, clean sans-serif for body copy. - Tone: boutique luxury real estate — editorial, refined, confident. Think high-end real estate magazine. - The Limitless Group logo appears on every graphic. Use the file I uploaded — never generate a version. - My contact info appears where it makes sense for the post type. Use only my real details above — never placeholder text. Default size is 1080x1080px. I'll share each request one at a time.

When ChatGPT goes off-brand — what to say
🔵
Wrong colors appeared
Blue, green, purple, or anything not in the palette.
"Please revise — remove all colors except warm white (#faf8f5), warm gold (#9b8c6e), and deep charcoal (#1a1a1a)."
⬜
Plain or solid background
ChatGPT generated a flat color background with no home photo.
"Please redo this with a real estate interior or exterior as the background. If it's too busy behind the text, blur it or apply a soft overlay."
🖼️
Logo is missing or rendering oddly
Most common cause: uploading the logo as a transparent PNG. Upload a version with a white or cream background instead. To fix mid-session, use the inline pencil editor — select the logo area and say: "Replace this with the Limitless Group logo from my uploaded file."
Prevention: screenshot the logo from any Limitless marketing PDF — it will have a background already.
👤
My info shows as placeholder text
Name, phone, or handle appears as "[AGENT NAME]" or similar.
"Replace all placeholder text with my real info from our session setup: [your name], [your phone], [your email], @mylimitlessagent."
📰
Looks too generic or template-y
Result looks like a stock social media template, not luxury real estate.
"This feels too generic. Make it more editorial — think high-end real estate magazine. Refine the typography, add breathing room, and remove anything decorative that isn't serving the design."

Making edits — the inline image editor
Use the ChatGPT pencil tool for finishing touches

When a graphic is close but needs a small fix — wrong handle, logo placement, a stat — don't regenerate the whole thing. Use the inline image editor to fix only what needs changing.

  • Hover over the generated image and tap the pencil icon ✏️
  • In the editor, select the Select tool (cursor/lasso icon)
  • Draw a selection around only the area you want to fix
  • Type your instruction — e.g. "Change this to @mylimitlessagent" or "Replace the logo with the Limitless Group logo from my uploaded file"
  • ChatGPT edits only the selected area — everything else stays exactly as is

💡 This is the recommended workflow for all finishing touches. Faster and more precise than regenerating.

Pro tips
01
One step at a time
Each workflow is designed to be sent one step at a time. Always let ChatGPT finish generating before sending the next message — it works sequentially.
02
Let it do the research
For neighborhood spotlights, market updates, and educational carousels — trust ChatGPT to research and write the content. That's where it really shines. Always verify stats before posting.
03
Stories are one line away
After any feed graphic, say: "Now create the same design at 1080x1920px for Instagram Stories." ChatGPT will recompose it — you don't have to start over.
04
Ask for 2 variations upfront
"Show me 2 layout options for this" gives you choices without multiple rounds of revision. Pick whichever is closest, then refine from there.
05
Save your best prompts
When a workflow produces a great result, note any tweaks you made. Over time, the team builds a Limitless-specific prompt library that gets better with every use.
06
Logo tip — always use a background
Upload the Limitless logo with a white or cream background, not as a transparent PNG. Transparent files cause rendering issues. Screenshot the logo from any Limitless marketing material for the easiest fix.
07
ChatGPT cuts out your headshot automatically
For any graphic requiring your cutout — listings, sold, open house, agent intro — just attach your regular headshot with its background. ChatGPT removes it and composites you seamlessly. If an edge looks rough, use the inline pencil editor to select and clean that area.
